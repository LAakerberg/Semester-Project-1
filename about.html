About us

The Community Science Museum was started back in 1990 by two of them must-know footballers in Australia. Their interest in history and that everyone should be able to learn and develop, according to them, is the most important thing they can give back to their own country.

Inside the museum you can find everything from the human body, space and back to the dinosaur time and much more. We have collected products from the must have famous exhibitions around the world.

Together with several famous artists and actors in Australia, the Future Museum has become one of the country's and the world's largest museums and is the most visited museum of all our tourists.

The most visited and popular exhibitions are clearly our space department. Here we have collected several famous objects over the years that have been and several famous astronauts have visited and given several lectures for our visitors.
